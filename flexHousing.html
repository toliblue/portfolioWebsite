Flex haus

A housing project designed to accommodate a wide variety of living situations, in particular co-housing and multi-generational living situations. Each unit can be customized by trading space with adjacent units. Each apartmant starts with a base unit of one living area and kitchen area, 3-4 bedrooms, and 2 bathrooms. Bedrooms units can be traded to extend an apartment up to 10 bedrooms.
